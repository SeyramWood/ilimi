Programs & Activities

Business Innovation Program:

The Business Innovation Programme focuses on and works with Young Leaders running profit-oriented initiatives. The iiLAB provides coaching and mentoring to help young leaders to develop their entrepreneurial projects, and later transform them into sustainable start-ups. Additionally, the Business Innovation Program encompasses incubation and pre-incubation program to cater to the peculiar needs of Young Leaders with existing projects and those who are in the process of developing their entrepreneurial idea. Here at the Business Innovation Program, we enjoy creativity and innovation and therefore advocate for creative ways of support through design thinking workshops, lean start-up method and MIT 24 steps.

Social Innovation and Change Initiative Program:

The Social Innovation and Change Initiative (SICI) is the social program of the incubator. Initiated in 2022, SICI focuses predominantly on women's mentoring programs and support of Young Leaders running non-profit organizations. The SICI team coaches and mentors its Young Leaders coordinates the voluntary community service and stirs numerous social initiatives which positively impact the wider community.

To accompany and support the Young Leaders on their entrepreneurial journey, the iiLab offers a range of services and activities including:

  • free co-working space;
  • financial support;
  • assistance with funding opportunities;
  • coaching and mentoring sessions;
  • guest lectures;
  • workshops (i.e. Design Thinking); and
  • events.

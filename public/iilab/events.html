Ilimi Annual Innovation Awards

The Ilimi Annual Innovation Awards 2022 was held on the Demo Day of the iiLab Kagame Program, on Friday January 21st, 2022. The ceremony was dedicated to entrepreneurs who distinguish themselves not only by their entrepreneurial results and impact on their communities but also by the hard work they put in to surpass themselves. These latter aspects fit perfectly with our ilimi values of leadership, excellence, and innovation. That is why we decided to celebrate these Young Leaders, also with a hope to inspire future generations. In total, seven prizes were awarded.

Business Innovation Awards:
  • Ilimi Entrepreneur of the Year
  • Most Innovative Entrepreneur of the Year
  • Emerging Entrepreneur of the Year
Social Innovation Awards:
  • Ilimi Social Innovator of the Year
  • Community Project of the Year
  • Community Leader of the Year
